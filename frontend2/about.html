How did we do it?


Convolutional Neural Networks is the hot new thing for machine learning. With the mass reduction in error rates for image processing due to CNN’s, CNN’s have become a powerful tool in today’s world. Companies all around the world use CNN’s including Google and even Instagram! But what are they? Here we will briefly explain what they are using it’s most known and popular use case, image processing. Specifically how we used image processing to solve our digit recognition problem.
